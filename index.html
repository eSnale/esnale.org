Welcome!

eSnale offers monthly pencil-and-paper puzzle packs to inmates in the state of Colorado, and our goal is to distribute to prison facilities throughout the rest of the country as well.  Each pack is $5.00, which covers shipping, and contains 36 double-sided sheets, including instructions.  Please limit orders to one pack per inmate per month, and feel free to contact us with any questions.

Step 1: Choose Puzzles

Any number of puzzle types you choose will be distributed as evenly as possible among the 36-sheet puzzle pack.  Please note that some puzzles are not available in every difficulty.  Click on any of the puzzle names for a printable free sample!
